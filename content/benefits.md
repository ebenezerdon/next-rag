# Employee Benefits

## Health Insurance

Full-time employees are eligible for health insurance starting the first of the month following their start date. We offer three plans: Basic, Standard, and Premium. The company covers 80% of premiums for the Standard plan.

## Dental and Vision

Dental and vision coverage is included with all health insurance plans at no additional cost. Annual eye exams and two dental cleanings per year are fully covered.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

Employees can contribute to the company 401(k) plan after 90 days of employment. The company matches 100% of contributions up to 4% of salary. Vesting is immediate.

## Life Insurance

Basic life insurance equal to one year's salary is provided at no cost. Additional coverage up to 5x salary can be purchased at group rates.

## Professional Development

Each employee receives $1,500 annually for professional development. This can be used for courses, certifications, conferences, or books. Unused funds do not roll over.

## Employee Assistance Program

Free confidential counseling services are available 24/7 through our EAP provider. Services include mental health support, financial counseling, and legal consultations.

## Commuter Benefits

Pre-tax commuter benefits are available for public transit and parking expenses up to IRS limits.
