# Time Off Policies

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

New employees receive 15 days of PTO per year. After 3 years, this increases to 20 days. After 7 years, employees receive 25 days. PTO accrues monthly and can be carried over up to 5 days per year.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 sick days per year. Sick leave can be used for personal illness or to care for immediate family members. A doctor's note is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

## Holidays

The company observes 10 paid holidays per year: New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, day after Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, and Christmas Day.

## Parental Leave

New parents receive 12 weeks of paid parental leave. This applies to birth, adoption, or foster placement. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event.

## Bereavement

Up to 5 days of paid bereavement leave is provided for the death of an immediate family member. Up to 3 days is provided for extended family members.

## Jury Duty

Employees receive full pay during jury duty service. Please provide your jury summons to HR as soon as possible.

## Requesting Time Off

All time off requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance when possible. Requests are approved based on business needs and team coverage.
