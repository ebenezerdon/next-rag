# Company Policies

## Work Hours

Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Employees are expected to work 40 hours per week. Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week after completing their 90-day probationary period. Remote work requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 24 hours in advance. A stable internet connection and appropriate workspace are required.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times. This includes treating colleagues with respect, maintaining confidentiality of company information, and adhering to all workplace safety guidelines.

## Dress Code

Business casual attire is required in the office. On Fridays, casual dress is permitted. When meeting with clients, business professional attire is expected.

## Communication

Email should be checked at least twice daily. Urgent matters should be communicated via Slack or phone. Response time for non-urgent emails is 24 business hours.

## Equipment

Company laptops and equipment must be used primarily for work purposes. Personal use is permitted in moderation. All equipment must be returned upon termination of employment.
