# Alien Flora: A Guide

## The Singing Moss of Xylos
Found in the damp caves of Xylos, this moss emits a soft hum when approached.
- **Danger Level**: Low.
- **Uses**: Great for lullabies.
- **Note**: Do not eat. It tastes like regret.

## The Carnivorous Sun-Flower
Native to the scorched plains of Helios Prime.
- **Appearance**: Looks like a normal sunflower, but with teeth.
- **Behavior**: Tracks the sun, and also anything made of meat.
- **Advice**: Keep your distance.

## Glow-Shrooms
Common in the underdark of many planets.
- **Properties**: Bioluminescent.
- **Edibility**: Some are delicious, some will make you see time backwards. Consult a local guide.
